## Key Insights from Bill's Experience:

### What Works:
1. **High-touch approach**: Multiple weekly touchpoints
2. **ADHD-specific solutions**: Not generic business advice
3. **Community element**: Peer support crucial for retention
4. **Energy/focus management**: Core differentiator from other coaches
5. **Self-trust building**: Addresses deeper psychological needs

### What to Avoid:
1. **Launch dependency**: Build recurring revenue models
2. **Unsustainable delivery**: 10-12 hour days don't scale
3. **Generic positioning**: "Business coaching" too broad
4. **Pricing too low**: Value-based pricing essential
5. **Ignoring churn**: Retention must be built into the model

### Pricing Philosophy:
- Start affordable but increase value rapidly
- Payment plans for mid-tier packages
- Focus on ROI and transformation, not hours
- Premium pricing for premium outcomes
- Multiple price points to serve different business stages
