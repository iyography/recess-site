## Content Guidelines

### Voice & Tone
- **Authentic**: Real stories, honest struggles, genuine wins
- **Relatable**: Uses ADHD-specific language and experiences
- **Encouraging**: Celebrates neurodivergent traits as strengths
- **Practical**: Provides actionable advice and concrete strategies
- **Community-focused**: Builds belonging and connection

### ADHD-Specific Elements to Include
- Hyperfocus and attention cycles
- Executive dysfunction experiences
- Rejection sensitivity stories
- Time blindness and energy management
- Pattern recognition and creativity
- Emotional intensity and authenticity
- Masking and unmasking journeys
- Neurodivergent strengths in business

### Content Quality Standards
- **Short version**: Punchy, social media ready, key insights
- **Medium version**: More context and practical application
- **Long version**: Complete story with detailed strategies and insights
- **Real examples**: Specific scenarios and concrete results
- **Actionable advice**: Clear next steps readers can implement
